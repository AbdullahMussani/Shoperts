OUR STORY

Our Story

Born from personal experience, Shoperts was founded by Mariam, a second-generation immigrant in North America. Mariam's own struggles to find reliable vendors in Pakistan while living abroad sparked the idea for a solution that would cater to the growing needs of the South Asian community.

As a fellow South Asian herself, Mariam understands the deep-rooted connection to our cultural heritage, especially when it comes ethnic products. She's witnessed firsthand the increasing demand for culturally inspired products among her friends and family, from clothing and accessories to home goods and wedding items. This growing desire to bring a piece of "home" to Canada inspired Mariam to create a platform that would connect South Asians with the best of Pakistani craftsmanship.

With fond memories of visiting Pakistan, Mariam has always admired the country's immense talent and potential. Despite the challenges faced by the nation, she believes in the power of its people to create beautiful and meaningful products.

Shoperts is more than just a shopping platform; it's a bridge between tradition and modernity. Our mission is to make it effortless for you to access the best of Pakistani fashion, no matter where you are.

Beyond providing a convenient shopping experience, Shoperts is committed to empowering women. Mariam is passionate about creating opportunities for women in visible minority groups to unleash their untapped potential. By supporting Pakistani artisans and businesses, Shoperts aims to contribute to the economic empowerment of women in Canada and Pakistan.

Our Mission

To empower the South Asian communities in Canada by providing a seamless and personalized shopping experience for Pakistani fashion. Our commitment to expert guidance, quality assurance, and convenient delivery ensures a delightful journey from selection to doorstep

Our Vision

To be the leading consultancy platform connecting the South Asian communities in Canada with the vibrant world of Pakistani fashion. We strive to offer an unparalleled shopping experience that seamlessly blends tradition, quality, and convenience.
